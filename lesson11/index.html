Soda springs mountain resort

As the longest-running ski resort in California, Soda Springs Mountain Resort is the best place to explore the magic of winter in Tahoe. Secluded in picturesque pine trees and conveniently located directly off Interstate 80, the welcoming resort spirit and laid-back atmosphere are perfect for families new to the snow.

In fact, Soda Springs offers Mountain Adventure access to Planet Kids, a dedicated snow play area designed for children under the age of 8. Although, big kids can play too! Planet Kids is best described as a snow playground with tube carousels, snow tube lanes, ski / snowboard specific learning areas, climbing snow volcanoes, and more.

If you're looking to speed things up a bit, Tube Town is Tahoe's premier snow tubing area with up to 20 slopes and 400 feet. surface elevation.
